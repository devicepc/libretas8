Anyone can review their points, to see what situation they are in. In case you do not have the notebook with you, you should ask a traffic police or the nearest Intendancy

In case of losing all your points, the book and the driver will be disabled to circulate.
And they must take the course indicated by the Intendancy or by tel. 111111111

You must have a valid username and password to enter the system of fines and courses

The Points are recovered by completing one of the courses indicated by the Administration and in one of the authorized centers
