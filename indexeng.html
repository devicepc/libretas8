In addition, if a driver commits two very serious offenses in a period of five years, he will receive the suspension of his driving license for one year. It is considered a repeat offense to commit the same offense in less than a year. In this case the penalty is double.

Apparently according to the law points are not always lost. The penalty scale has first the warning, then the fine, then the loss of points, the temporary suspension of the permit, the cancellation of the permit, the removal of the car's license plates, until reaching the immobilization or removal of the vehicle. The real project (included in article 23) creates a single national driving license, with a certain number of points.

The new traffic law says that the departmental governments will carry out “the control and supervision on public roads and driving practices to obtain the Single National Driving Permit, in the entire departmental territory, either on national or departmental routes. , without prejudice to the current competences of the national organisms”. If you have a passbook without this system, at the time of renewing it, the user will be given immediate entry with their corresponding points.
